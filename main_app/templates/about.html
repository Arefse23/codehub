FAQ

What is CodeHub?

CodeHub is your one-stop destination to debugging your faulty code, and forming communities with other like-minded developers.


How do you access CodeHub?

Sign up, sign in, and you're ready to explore CodeHub.


How is CodeHub used?

If you have any errors in your code, or are facing any technical issues you can post a question in CodeHub, and attach the faulty code. Community members will get back to you with their solutions.


How do I know which answer is the correct one?

Community members will be able to 'like' people's answers. Generally, the more likes a solution has the more reliable it is deemed to be. The answer with most likes will be pinned at the top and highlighted.
